# *Salvation and the Risen King*

*Assurance, Allegiance, and Life in the Kingdom*

*Salvation and the Risen King* explores the nature of salvation through the lens of union with Christ and the kingship of Jesus.

The book argues that salvation is participation in the life of the risen King through faith understood as trusting allegiance.

## Chapter Outline

1. The Gospel of the Risen King  
2. Union with the Risen King  
3. Union with the King and His Kingdom  
4. Faith as Allegiance to the King  
5. Assurance in the Life of the King  
6. Living Under the Reign of the King  
7. Persevering with the King  

## About the Book

This project develops a kingdom-centered understanding of salvation grounded in union with Christ.  
Essays published on this site explore themes that will appear in the book.
